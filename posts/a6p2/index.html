Objects to Perceive

The main object we will need to proceed for our project is human hands (for leading humans around). It may also be useful to track doorknobs or elevator buttons to increase the range of autonomous movement.

If we increase the project scope, we could also include picking up or manipulating additional objects, such as:

  • Wallets
  • Keys
  • Nametags

But these are tangential to our primary goal.
