Interactive Marker Demonstration

This video demonstrates the use of interactive markers for robot control in RViz. We also use non-interactive markers to show the path traced by the robot's movement (the white line). The arrow-with-ring interactive marker is used to specify the desired position and orientation for the robot, and a click on the blue cube marker (repurposed from lab 13) sends the robot to that pose. Markers can be used to control both position (by dragging the arrow) and rotation (by dragging the surrounding ring).
