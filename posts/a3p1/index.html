Interface Explanation:

Our interface includes the following components:

  • Camera View: The RGB camera feed from the robot's head-mounted camera
  • Torso Height: The current height of the robot's torso, in meters
  • Base Control: Motion control of the base, including forward/back (translation) and left/right (rotation) buttons. The robot moves at a fixed rate while the buttons are held down.
  • Gripper Control: Buttons to open and close the gripper at the end of the robot's arm
  • Head Control: Sliders to control the head tilt and pan angles (and the camera view)
  • Arm Control: Sliders to control the arm joint angles

We made sure that all control buttons would be visible without having to scroll down the page, making the teleoperation process easier.
